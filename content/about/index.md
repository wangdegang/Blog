## About Me
**_Degang Wang_** is a real-world data (RWD) and analytics researcher with over 17 years of experience transforming data into decision-grade evidence. His work sits at the intersection of epidemiology, statistical methodology, and modern data platforms, with a focus on turning complex real-world data into actionable insights.

His expertise spans RWD source evaluation, study design and execution, and the development of scalable, cloud-native analytics infrastructure. He is particularly interested in how rigorous methodology and advanced analytics can be combined to generate credible evidence that informs clinical and business decisions.

Degang currently serves as a Director in the HEOR Epidemiology and Real-World Evidence CoE at [Regeneron](https://www.regeneron.com/). Prior to this, he held roles at [Baylor College of Medicine](https://www.houston.hsrd.research.va.gov/), [Truven Health Analytics](https://www.merative.com/truven) (now part of Merative), [Amgen](https://www.amgen.com/), and [Allergan](https://www.abbvie.com/) (now part of Abbvie), building experience across academic, consulting, and industry settings.

He holds a PhD in Molecular Genetics and an MS in Biostatistics from [The University of Texas Health Science Center at Houston](https://www.uth.edu/), as well as a BM (MBBS equivalent) in Preventive Medicine from [Norman Bethune University of Medical Sciences](https://jdyxb.jlu.edu.cn/) (now part of Jilin University) in China.

Based in Houston, Texas, Degang enjoys road trips with his family, often exploring new places beyond the data.
